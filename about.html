Boost your productivity and build your mood with a glass of coffee in the morning. Coffee has stimulating effect. Cold brew is a method of making coffee by steeping coarsely ground coffee beans in cold or room-temperature water for an extended period, typically 12 to 24 hours. The result is a smooth, less acidic, and naturally sweet coffee concentrate that can be served in various ways.
